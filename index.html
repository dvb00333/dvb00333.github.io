Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Spaghetti Noodles1 box
Avocado1 medium
Pine Nuts1 cup
Olive Oil2 tablespoons
Basil2 cups
Salt and Pepperto your liking
Cherry Tomatoes1 pint

Instructions

  • Cook pasta according to instructions on the box
  • Using a food processor, blend the avocado, pine nuts, olive oil, basil, salt, and pepper
  • Once the mixture is a paste-like consistency, taste it and adjust seasoning accordingly
  • Toss the pesto with the pasta
  • Add cherry tomatoes
  • Enjoy!!!
